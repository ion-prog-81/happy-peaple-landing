HP-HPP

Hydrolyzed Pea Protein

Enzymatically hydrolyzed pea protein for crystal-clear beverages, sports nutrition and juice fortification. High solubility across all pH levels.

💧

Product Overview

Happy Pea Hydro Protein (HP-HPP) is produced through controlled enzymatic hydrolysis, breaking pea protein into small peptides (500–2,000 Da). The result: exceptional solubility across all pH levels, crystal-clear solutions, and rapid absorption — ideal for premium sports nutrition and functional beverages.

💧 Crystal Clear

Transparent solution at all pH levels — no cloudiness or sedimentation.

⚡ Fast Absorption

Small peptides rapidly absorbed — perfect for post-workout recovery.

🍋 Acid Stable

Remains soluble even at low pH — compatible with fruit juices.

😌 Neutral Flavour

Significantly less beany than standard isolate — cleaner taste.
