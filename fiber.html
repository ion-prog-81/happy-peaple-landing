Pea Fiber

Our pea fiber is a clean-label dietary fiber concentrate produced from the outer hull of yellow peas. Available in three grades: Fiber 40 (standard), Fiber 60 (high-fiber), and Fiber Fine (ultra-fine, 80–100 mesh) for premium applications demanding smooth texture. Naturally rich in insoluble and soluble fiber, it supports digestive health claims and clean-label positioning.

🌿 Clean Label

Simple pea ingredient — no chemicals, no additives. Consumer-friendly.

📊 Three Grades

40%, 60% and Fine (80–100 mesh) — flexibility for any application.

🥖 Texture Improvement

Improves moisture retention and mouthfeel in baked goods and meat products.

🥗 Nutritional Boost

Increases dietary fiber content for health-focused product positioning.

pea fiber

Happy Pea Hydro Protein (HP-PF-40 / HP-PF-60 / HP-PF-FINE) is produced through controlled enzymatic hydrolysis, breaking pea protein into small peptides (500–2,000 Da). The result: exceptional solubility across all pH levels, crystal-clear solutions, and rapid absorption — ideal for premium sports nutrition and functional beverages.

💧 Crystal Clear

Transparent solution at all pH levels — no cloudiness, no sedimentation.

⚡ Fast Absorption

Small peptides are rapidly absorbed by the body — perfect for post-workout recovery.

🍋 Acid Stable

Remains soluble even at low pH — compatible with fruit juices and citrus beverages.

😌 Neutral Flavour

Significantly less beany than standard isolate — cleaner taste profile.
