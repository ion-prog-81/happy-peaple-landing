HP-PP-80 · HP-PP-85

Pea Protein Isolate 80/85

High-purity pea protein isolate for meat alternatives, dairy analogs, sports nutrition and nutritional supplements. Excellent emulsification, neutral flavour, consistent quality.

Product Overview

Our pea protein isolate is produced from high-quality yellow split peas through a gentle wet-fractionation process that preserves functional properties while delivering 80–85% protein content. Non-GMO, allergen-free, and sustainably sourced.

🔬 High Purity

Protein ≥80% (HP-PP-80) or ≥85% (HP-PP-85). Low fat, low carbohydrates.

🌱 Allergen-Free

No soy, no gluten, no dairy, no nuts. Clean label friendly.

⚗️ Excellent Emulsification

Strong water and oil binding capacity for superior texture in meat and dairy alternatives.

🌍 Traceable Supply

Full chain traceability from Qingdao, China. FOB, CIF, DDP terms available.
