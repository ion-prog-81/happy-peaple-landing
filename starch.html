HP-PS-N · HP-PS-PG

Happy Pea Starch

Functional pea starch for texturizing, binding and film-forming in snacks, confectionery and processed foods. Two grades available to suit your process.

Product Overview

Pea starch is a valuable co-product of pea protein extraction. Our pea starch offers excellent functional properties including high viscosity, good film-forming ability and a clean, neutral taste. Available as native starch (HP-PS-N) for standard applications and pregelatinized starch (HP-PS-PG) for cold-process formulations.

🌾 Clean Taste

Neutral, non-cereal flavour profile — won't compete with your formulation.

💪 Strong Gel Formation

High viscosity and good freeze-thaw stability for consistent texture.

🧬 Two Grades

Native (HP-PS-N) for cooked systems and Pregel (HP-PS-PG) for cold processes.

🌍 Allergen-Free

Naturally gluten-free, non-GMO, suitable for clean-label products.
